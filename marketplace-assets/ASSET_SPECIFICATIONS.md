# Marketplace Asset Specifications

## Required Assets
1. `logo.png`
   - Format: PNG
   - Minimum size: 100x100
   - Style: Professional, clear on light and dark backgrounds

2. Screenshots (all PNG, 1280x720)
   - `screenshot-1-dashboard.png` (Dashboard overview)
   - `screenshot-2-revenue.png` (Revenue metrics)
   - `screenshot-3-governance.png` (Finance governance)
   - `screenshot-4-proof.png` (Delivery proof)
   - `screenshot-5-pricing.png` (Pricing page)

## Location
Save all assets under `marketplace-assets/`.
