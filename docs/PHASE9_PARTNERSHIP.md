## Part 1: Stripe Partnership Framework

### Stripe Partnership Tiers

Stripe recognizes partner apps by tier. Understand where you fit:

| Tier | Criteria | Benefits | Timeline |
|------|----------|----------|----------|
| **Registered App** | Listed in marketplace | Basic listing | Automatic (you're here) |
| **Featured App** | >100 installs, 4.0+ rating, strong reviews | Homepage visibility, marketing | 6-12 months |
| **Technology Partner** | Proven integration, revenue >$10K/mo, official Stripe partnership | Co-marketing budget, developer relations | 12-24 months |
| **Ecosystem Partner** | Revenue >$100K/mo, Stripe revenue share >$50K/mo, strategic alignment | CEO-level relationship, co-development | 24+ months |

**Your current path:** Registered App → Featured App (6-12 months) → Technology Partner (12-24 months)

### Stripe's Partner Program

**What Stripe provides (at each tier):**

**Registered App (You):**
- ✅ Listing in Stripe Marketplace
- ✅ Access to Stripe community forums
- ✅ Technical support (standard)

**Featured App (Goal for Month 6-12):**
- ✅ All of above, plus:
- ✅ Featured listing on marketplace homepage
- ✅ Mention in Stripe monthly newsletter
- ✅ Case study opportunity
- ✅ Developer relations contact

**Technology Partner (Goal for Year 2):**
- ✅ All of above, plus:
- ✅ Joint marketing budget ($10K-50K/year)
- ✅ Co-authored content (blog, webinars)
- ✅ Revenue share program (15-30% of your Stripe fees)
- ✅ Dedicated Stripe partner manager
- ✅ Priority technical support

**To become Featured App:**
- Installs: >100
- Rating: ≥4.0/5.0
- Reviews: ≥5 positive reviews
- Documentation: Complete and helpful
- Support: <24 hour response time

**To become Technology Partner:**

## Part 5: Revenue Share Models

### What to Expect

**Stripe typically offers:**

**Tier 1 (Standard for new apps):** No direct revenue share. You charge customers separately.
- You keep 100% of revenue
- Stripe takes payment processing fees (2.9% + $0.30)
- Mutual benefit: You drive Stripe volume

**Tier 2 (Featured/Technology Partner):** Revenue share on Stripe fees you generate
- You receive: 15-30% of Stripe fees you drive
- Example: You charge customer $1000/month. Stripe volume = $100K/month. Stripe fees = $2,900. Your share = $435-870/month
- Requires: >$10K/month Stripe volume

**Tier 3 (Ecosystem Partner):** Higher revenue share + co-development budget
- You receive: 25-50% of incremental Stripe fees
- Plus: $50K-500K annual co-marketing budget
- Requires: >$100K/month Stripe volume + strategic alignment

### Your Revenue Model Options

**Option A: SaaS Subscription (Recommended for Year 1)**
- Freemium: Free (up to 100 ops/month)
- Pro: $99/month (unlimited operations)
- Enterprise: Custom pricing

**How it works with Stripe:**
- Your customers charge you (via Stripe) $99/month
- Stripe takes 2.9% + $0.30 = ~$3 per subscription
- You keep $96
- As you grow, negotiate revenue share on the Stripe volume your customers drive

**Option B: Per-Operation Pricing**
- $0.05-0.10 per gated operation
- Scales with customer volume
- Better for high-volume customers

**Option C: Revenue Share on Customer Stripe Volume**
- Customer generates $100K/month in Stripe charges
- You take 0.5-2% of that volume
- Only works for payment processors and high-volume platforms

**Option D: Hybrid**
- Base subscription ($50-99/month) + per-operation overages ($0.02 per op)
- Best balance of revenue and usage-based pricing

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

❌ **Pitching too early:** Wait until 25+ customers and 4.0+ rating  
❌ **Asking for money upfront:** Propose mutual benefit first  
❌ **Unfocused integrations:** Do 3 well, not 10 poorly  
❌ **Overselling partnership value:** Be honest about traction  
❌ **Ignoring customer needs:** Only partner if it helps your users  
❌ **Exclusive relationships:** Never agree to Stripe exclusivity (competitors exist)  
❌ **Resource drain:** Don't let partnerships distract from product
