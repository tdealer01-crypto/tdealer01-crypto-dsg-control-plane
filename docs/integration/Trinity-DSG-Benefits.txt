EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

This integration brings 7-agent orchestration (Phase 5) into Trinity's job
workflow control plane. Result: real-time visibility, cost governance, audit
trails, and production-ready monitoring.

Key Outcome: Trinity gets full agent visibility + control, enabling $150k/year
operational savings through faster debugging, cost control, and reliability.
