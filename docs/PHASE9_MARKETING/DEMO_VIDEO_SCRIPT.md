# Demo Video Script: DSG Governance Gate for Stripe

## Overview
- **Duration:** 90 seconds
- **Format:** Screen share + narrator voiceover
- **Tone:** Professional, clear, action-focused
- **Audience:** Finance/payment teams, decision-makers, technical operators
- **Video Platform:** YouTube, LinkedIn, company website, Stripe Marketplace

---

## Pre-Production Notes

### Equipment Needed
- Desktop/laptop with Stripe Dashboard access
- DSG Control Plane interface (test environment)
- Screen recording software (OBS, ScreenFlow, Camtasia)
- Voiceover narration (professional voiceover artist or clear speaker)
- Optional: B-roll library for transitions/atmosphere

### Color/Branding
- Use DSG brand colors for highlights
- Ensure text is legible at 720p+ resolution
- Use sans-serif fonts (Arial, Helvetica, Inter)

### Audio
- Professional background music (royalty-free, subtle)
- Clear voiceover (no background noise)
- Sound effects for key interactions (optional, use sparingly)

---

## Scene-by-Scene Breakdown

### Scene 1: Problem Intro (0-8 seconds)

**Video:**
- Screen: Stripe Dashboard showing transaction list
- Highlight one transaction marked "Flagged for Review"
- Zoom in on "Reason: Manual Review Required"

**Voiceover:**
"Every day, payment platforms face the same challenge: how do you catch risky transactions *before* they become problems?"

**Key Visual:** Red exclamation mark or "at-risk" badge appears on a transaction

**Talking Points:**
- Establish the problem (risky transactions slip through)
- Show real Stripe interface
- Emphasize "before they become problems"

---

### Scene 2: Current Pain Point (8-16 seconds)

**Video:**
- Screen: Task list showing "Review 47 flagged transactions" or similar backlog
- Show a human reviewer manually reading transaction details
- Slow pan across multiple transactions

**Voiceover:**
"Most teams rely on manual review. But manual review doesn't scale. Compliance rules change constantly. Audit trails are incomplete. And one missed transaction can become a costly incident."

**Key Visual:** Animated counter showing transaction backlog growing

**Talking Points:**
- Manual bottleneck
- Compliance complexity
- Lack of audit trails
- Business impact

---

### Scene 3: Solution Introduction (16-24 seconds)

**Video:**
- Transition: Fade to DSG Control Plane dashboard
- Show the DSG Governance Gate icon/logo
- Animated text: "DSG Governance Gate"

**Voiceover:**
"Introducing DSG Governance Gate—the policy engine that brings deterministic, provable governance to Stripe."

**Key Visual:** DSG logo + "Governance Gate" title with subtle animation

**Talking Points:**
- Name and core concept (policy engine)
- Deterministic = reproducible, provable
- Governance = controlled risk

---

### Scene 4: Policy Definition (24-40 seconds)

**Video:**
- Screen: Policy editor showing a sample policy
  - Example: "Block any transaction over $10,000 without 3D Secure"
  - Show simple, human-readable syntax
- Cursor highlights key policy elements (threshold, condition, action)

**Voiceover:**
"First, you define your policies in plain language. 'Block transactions over $10,000 without 3D Secure.' 'Flag high-volume sellers on their first day.' Rules are clear. No ambiguity."

**Key Visual:** Color-coded policy breakdown (conditions in green, actions in red, thresholds in blue)

**Talking Points:**
- Policies are human-readable, not complex code
- Examples are specific and relevant
- Easy to understand and audit

---

### Scene 5: Real-Time Evaluation (40-56 seconds)

**Video:**
- Screen: Incoming transaction appears
- Show transaction details (amount, payment method, merchant, risk indicators)
- Policy engine "thinking" animation (subtle)
- Three outcome badges appear:
  - Green "APPROVE"
  - Red "BLOCK"
  - Yellow "REVIEW"

**Voiceover:**
"When a transaction arrives, it runs through your policies in milliseconds. The outcome is always one of three: Approve—meets all policies. Block—violates a policy. Or Review—edge case, needs human judgment."

**Key Visual:**
- Transaction data flows into policy engine (animated arrows)
- Engine processes (brief spinner or progress bar)
- Decision appears with clear badge/color

**Talking Points:**
- Real-time processing
- Three clear outcomes
- Deterministic (no guessing)

---

### Scene 6: Audit Trail (56-72 seconds)

**Video:**
- Screen: Audit log showing a sample decision
  - Decision: BLOCK
  - Policy Applied: "SCA Policy v2.1"
  - Reason: "3D Secure verification failed"
  - Timestamp: [exact time]
  - Policy Hash: [proof hash]

**Voiceover:**
"Every decision creates a complete audit trail. You can see exactly *why* a transaction was approved, blocked, or flagged—what policy was applied, what data was evaluated, and when. That's proof you can show auditors."

**Key Visual:**
- Audit entry highlighted
- Zoom in on key fields (Policy, Reason, Proof Hash)
- Optional: Show export to CSV/PDF

**Talking Points:**
- Complete traceability
- Compliance-ready evidence
- Auditor-friendly format

---

### Scene 7: Dashboard & Metrics (72-84 seconds)

**Video:**
- Screen: Control Plane dashboard showing:
  - Transaction volume graph (e.g., "1,247 transactions processed today")
  - Breakdown: Approve (90%), Block (5%), Review (5%)
  - Pie chart showing policy violations by type

**Voiceover:**
"Your entire governance program is visible in one dashboard. See how many transactions are approved, blocked, or flagged. Understand which policies matter most. Spot patterns before they become incidents."

**Key Visual:**
- Dashboard widgets update in real-time (optional animation)
- Use green/yellow/red color coding
- Keep charts simple and scannable

**Talking Points:**
- Real-time visibility
- Actionable metrics
- Risk identification

---

### Scene 8: CTA & Summary (84-90 seconds)

**Video:**
- Screen: Stripe Marketplace app page OR sign-up page
- Animated text: "Ready to control your payment risk?"
- Call-to-action button highlighted

**Voiceover:**
"DSG Governance Gate is now available on Stripe App Marketplace. Install in minutes. Define your first policy in hours. Start proving compliance today."

**Optional text on screen:**
- "Free 30-day trial"
- "No credit card required"
- "dsg.pics/stripe-governance"

**Key Visual:** Clear, clickable CTA button

**Talking Points:**
- Product availability
- Ease of adoption
- Time-to-value
- Call-to-action link

   - Stock: Hands on keyboard, typing policies
   - Stock: Compliance documents, audit reports
   - Stock: Security/lock icons
   - Custom: Stripe Dashboard in use

2. **Transaction Processing Theme:**
   - Stock: Payment cards, transaction flows
   - Stock: Network/data center imagery
   - Stock: Real-time data processing visualization
   - Custom: Sample transaction from test environment

3. **Success/Growth Theme:**
   - Stock: Upward trending graphs
   - Stock: Team collaboration, meeting room
   - Stock: Checkmarks, approvals
   - Custom: Green APPROVE badges

4. **Risk/Caution Theme (for problem intro):**
   - Stock: Warning icons, caution symbols
   - Stock: Stressed team member (optional)
   - Custom: Red BLOCK badges
   - Custom: Flagged transaction highlight

## Voiceover & Tone Guidelines

### Delivery Style
- **Pace:** Moderate, conversational (not robotic)
- **Tone:** Confident, reassuring, professional
- **Energy:** Slightly elevated but calm (not salesy)
- **Emphasis:** Emphasize key benefits (control, compliance, speed)

### Voice Characteristics (Preferred)
- Male or female, age-neutral
- Clear enunciation (no heavy accent)
- Studio-quality audio
- Professional experience with fintech/B2B

## Accessibility Notes

- Provide accurate captions for all spoken content
- Describe visual elements for screen reader users
- Use high contrast colors (especially in dashboard shots)
- Avoid flashing or rapid scene transitions (seizure safety)
- Provide transcript alongside video
