# Email Launch Campaign: DSG Governance Gate for Stripe

## Campaign Overview
- **Duration:** 8 days (5 emails)
- **Target Audience:** Stripe users, payment operators, compliance teams, fintech decision-makers
- **Sending Schedule:** Day 0, 2, 4, 6, 8
- **Open Rate Target:** 35-40%
- **Click-Through Rate Target:** 8-12%
- **Conversion Goal:** App install + policy configuration

---

## Campaign Strategy

### Email List Segments
1. **Primary:** Stripe users with active integrations
2. **Secondary:** Fintech/payment platform operators
3. **Tertiary:** Compliance/risk management professionals

### Personalization Tokens
- `[FIRST_NAME]` — recipient's first name
- `[COMPANY_NAME]` — recipient's company
- `[TRANSACTION_VOLUME]` — estimated transaction volume (if available)
- `[INDUSTRY]` — recipient's industry (fintech, ecommerce, saas, etc.)

### Campaign Goals
- Email 1: Awareness (Day 0 announcement)
- Email 2: Education (what it does)
- Email 3: Engagement (real-world use cases)
- Email 4: Conversion (pricing, features, urgency)
- Email 5: Action (limited-time offer, final push)

## Testing & Optimization

### A/B Testing Plan
- **Email 1:** Test subject lines (50/50 split)
- **Email 2:** Test CTA button text (demo vs. marketplace)
- **Email 3:** Test use case relevance by industry segment
- **Email 4:** Test pricing model presentation (options vs. single plan)
- **Email 5:** Test discount percentage (40%, 50%, 60% off)

### Monitoring Cadence
- Open/click rates: measured hourly for first 24 hours
- Conversion rate: measured daily
- Opt-out/complaint rate: monitored continuously
- Winner declared: 24 hours after send (earliest)

### Backup Sends
If open rates drop below 20%:
- Resend Day 1 campaigns with new subject lines (24 hours later)
- Increase discount in Day 5 email if conversion rate <1%
- Add SMS component if segment is mobile-heavy
