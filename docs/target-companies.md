## Scoring Criteria

For each company, score on ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) fit:

| Factor | Points | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| **FinTech/SaaS** | 0–25 | Is company in target segment? |
| **Stripe Usage** | 0–25 | Do they likely use Stripe? |
| **Governance Need** | 0–20 | High refund/payout volume? |
| **Company Stage** | 0–15 | Series A+ preferred |
| **Team Size** | 0–15 | 20+ employees better |

**Scoring**: 80–100 = Very High Priority | 60–79 = High Priority | 40–59 = Medium Priority | <40 = Lower Priority

Most teams handle refunds/payouts reactively (they log them after execution). 
We built DSG to gate these operations *before* they execute.

One customer prevented a $100k accidental refund. Another reduced compliance 
audit time from 3 weeks to 2 days.

Worth a 15-min call to see if it fits your use case?

→ [Calendar link]

Best,
[Your Name]
DSG Governance
t.dealer01@dsg.pics
```

```
Hi [Name],

payouts/refunds/charges.

before they execute, generates audit trails for compliance.

security incident prevented.

Open to a quick chat?

[Calendar link]

## Notes
