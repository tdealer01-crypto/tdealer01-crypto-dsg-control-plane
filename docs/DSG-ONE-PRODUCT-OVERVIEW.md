## Core Value Proposition

### The Problem
- AI makes fast decisions, but you can't prove they followed policy
- You have logs, but not proof
- Regulators ask "who approved this?" and you have no answer
- You don't know what your AI operations actually cost

### The Solution
DSG ONE is the **accountability layer** for AI operations:

- **Every decision is verified** against your policies before execution
- **Every decision is recorded** with tamper-proof proof (SHA-256 hashing)
- **Every decision can be replayed** bit-for-bit years later
- **Every decision has an owner** (human approval trail)

## Technical Foundation

Under the hood, DSG ONE uses:
- **Z3 formal verification** — provably correct decision logic
- **SHA-256 hashing** — tamper-proof audit trail
- **Deterministic gates** — same input always = same output (replay-able)
- **CCVS L1-L5** — compliance evidence auto-generated

But you don't need to know this. Just write a policy, and DSG ONE handles the rest.
