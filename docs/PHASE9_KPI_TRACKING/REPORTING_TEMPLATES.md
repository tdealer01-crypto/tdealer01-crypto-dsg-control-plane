# Reporting Templates — Phase 9+ Stakeholder Communication

**Last Updated:** 2026-06-07  
**Purpose:** Provide ready-to-use templates for weekly status reports, monthly stakeholder updates, quarterly board reports, customer case studies, and partner performance reports.

---

## Overview

This document contains 5 reporting templates designed for different audiences and cadences:

1. **Weekly Status Report** — internal team, 5-min read
2. **Monthly Stakeholder Report** — advisors/investors, 10-min read
3. **Quarterly Board Update** — board/investors, 20-min read
4. **Customer Case Study** — marketing/testimonial, 5-page document
5. **Partner Performance Report** — integration partners, 10-min read

---

## Template 1: Weekly Status Report

**Audience:** Team (internal)  
**Frequency:** Every Friday @ 5 PM  
**Format:** Slack message or Google Doc  
**Length:** 200-300 words  

### Structure

```
# Weekly Status Report — Week of [DATE RANGE]

## 🎯 Week-at-a-Glance
[One sentence summary]

## 📊 Key Metrics (Week-over-Week Change)
[Table: DAU, Signups, MRR, Conversion, Uptime with status indicators]

## 🎉 Wins This Week
[3 bullet points of wins]

## ⚠️ Concerns / Blockers
[2-3 bullet points of issues]

## 🚀 Next Week Focus
[3 numbered action items with owners]

## 📈 Looking Ahead (2-3 Weeks)
[Upcoming events/launches]

## 💬 Feedback / Questions
[Open discussion]
```

## Template 2: Monthly Stakeholder Report

**Audience:** Advisors, angel investors, board members  
**Frequency:** First Monday of each month  
**Format:** Email + PDF attachment  
**Length:** 800-1000 words / 5-6 pages

### Sections

1. Executive Summary (1-2 paragraphs)
2. Key Numbers (4-5 headline metrics)
3. Progress Against OKRs (3 OKRs with status)
4. Metrics Dashboard (6 KPI tables by tier)
5. Cohort Analysis (customer quality by channel)
6. Product Development (launched, in progress, top requests)
7. Market & Competitive Insights (trends, competition, partnerships)
8. Team & Hiring (headcount, open roles, morale)
9. Financial Summary (P&L, burn, runway)
10. Key Decisions & Changes (why matters?)
11. Risks & Mitigations (top 3 risks with actions)
12. Asks / Feedback (what you need from advisors)
13. Outlook & Next Month (focus areas, milestones, contingencies)

## Template 3: Quarterly Board Update

**Audience:** Board of directors, major investors  
**Frequency:** Quarterly (end of each quarter)  
**Format:** Slide deck (15 slides) + 20-min presentation  
**Length:** 10-15 pages / 15 slides

### Slide Outline

- **Slides 1-2:** Title + Quarterly Themes & Highlights
- **Slides 3-5:** KPI Summary, Adoption Metrics (Tier 1), Engagement & Retention (Tier 2-3)
- **Slide 6:** Revenue & Unit Economics (Tier 4)
- **Slide 7:** Market & Competitive Context
- **Slide 8:** Product Roadmap (3-Month Outlook)
- **Slide 9:** Operational Health & Team
- **Slide 10:** Financial Snapshot (P&L, runway, burn trend)
- **Slide 11:** Top Risks & Mitigations
- **Slide 12:** Key Decisions Needed from Board
- **Slide 13:** Top 3 Focus Areas for Next Quarter
- **Slide 14:** Expected Milestones
- **Slide 15:** Questions & Closing

## Template 4: Customer Case Study

**Audience:** Marketing, prospective customers  
**Frequency:** 1-2 per quarter (after major milestones)  
**Format:** 2000-word PDF or blog post  
**Length:** 5-6 pages

### Structure

1. **Title & Customer Profile** (name, industry, size, key need)
2. **The Challenge** (customer's problem, pain points, impact)
3. **The Solution** (why they chose DSG ONE, implementation timeline, key features)
4. **The Results** (quantified metrics, customer quotes, outcomes)
5. **Key Learnings** (from customer and DSG ONE team)
6. **Expansion** (what's next, growth trajectory)
7. **Recommendation** (rating, endorsement quote)
8. **About [Customer]** (company overview)
9. **Call to Action** (free trial, demo, docs, contact sales)

## Template 5: Partner Performance Report

**Audience:** Integration partners (Vercel, GitHub, Anthropic, etc.)  
**Frequency:** Quarterly or semi-annual  
**Format:** Email + PDF  
**Length:** 3-5 pages

### Sections

1. Partnership Summary (overview, launch date, value prop)
2. Performance Metrics (customer count, adoption %, integrations, revenue influenced)
3. Customer Spotlights (2 example customers and their results)
4. Co-Marketing & Collaboration (completed activities, planned activities)
5. Roadmap Alignment (DSG ONE roadmap, Partner roadmap, integration opportunities)
6. Feedback & Requests (from customer voice, from partner)
7. Health Check (API integration status, documentation, support, revenue)
8. Opportunities & Next Steps (expansion ideas, discussion topics)
9. Quarterly Outlook (expected outcomes, key milestones)
10. Closing (next sync date, call to action)

## Content Guidelines for All Reports

### Tone & Style

- **Professional but approachable:** Use "we" language; acknowledge challenges; celebrate wins
- **Quantitative over qualitative:** Lead with numbers; explain the story behind them
- **Forward-looking:** Balance past performance with future outlook
- **Honest:** Include concerns, risks, and lessons learned; don't sugarcoat

### Data Sources

- **Vercel Analytics:** Traffic, user behavior, conversion funnels
- **Supabase:** User data, subscription info, feature usage, audit logs
- **Stripe:** Revenue, churn, customer data
- **Better Uptime:** API uptime %, response times, incidents
- **Customer interviews:** Feedback, satisfaction, case studies
- **Internal spreadsheets:** Burn rate, runway, team headcount
