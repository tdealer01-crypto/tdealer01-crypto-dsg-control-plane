## Finding Contacts (Per Company)

### Step 1: LinkedIn
- Search: "[Company Name] Engineering Manager" OR "VP Engineering"
- Filter: 50-500 employee companies
- Note: Seniority level, company tenure, recent activity

### Step 2: Email Discovery
- Use [RocketReach](rocketreach.com), [Hunter.io](hunter.io), [Clearbit](clearbit.com)
- Pattern: firstname@company.com, f.lastname@company.com, flastname@company.com
- Verify via LinkedIn before sending

### Step 3: Warm Intro
- Check your network (founders, investors, friends)
- Ask: "Do you know anyone at [Company] who cares about AI governance?"
- Use warm intro email (higher open rate)

### Step 4: Cold Email
- If no warm intro, use email template from `customer-acquisition.ts`
- Send from sales@dsg-platform.com (professional)
- Track opens via Pipedrive / HubSpot
