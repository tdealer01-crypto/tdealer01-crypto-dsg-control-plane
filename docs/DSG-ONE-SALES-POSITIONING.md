## The 4-Question Sales Framework

### Question 1: "What happened?"
- **Competitors:** We log it (if they remember to log it)
- **DSG ONE:** ✅ Dashboard shows every decision, when it happened, who approved it
- **Why:** Real-time visibility across all AI integrations (Stripe, OpenAI, GitHub, etc.)

### Question 2: "Did it follow our policy?"
- **Competitors:** We assume it did (hope for the best)
- **DSG ONE:** ✅ Policy gate enforced it before execution; proof of verification included
- **Why:** Prevents policy violations, reduces risk

### Question 3: "Who approved this risky decision?"
- **Competitors:** Nobody, it was automatic (problem for regulators)
- **DSG ONE:** ✅ Approval trail shows name, timestamp, reason; can replay exact approval workflow
- **Why:** Satisfies "human in the loop" regulatory requirement

### Question 4: "Can you prove this in an audit?"
- **Competitors:** We have logs... (hope they haven't changed)
- **DSG ONE:** ✅ Tamper-proof SHA-256 hash chain; replay any decision bit-for-bit years later
- **Why:** Auditors get proof, not just claims
