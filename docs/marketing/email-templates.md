## Copy Style Guide

### Tone
- **Honest**: Be direct about what DSG does (pre-execution gating, not post-execution logging)
- **Fast**: Use short sentences, bullet points, action-oriented language
- **Evidence-based**: Use real examples and numbers (not hype)
- **Helpful**: Lead with customer benefit, not product features

### Banned Phrases
- "revolutionary" (use "pre-execution" instead)
- "AI-powered" (we don't use AI, we use deterministic rules)
- "industry-leading" (unverified claim)
- "guaranteed compliance" (we provide proof, not guarantees)
- "world-class" (vague, meaningless)

### Preferred Phrases
- "Pre-execution governance" (clear, specific)
- "Cryptographic audit trails" (precise, auditor-ready)
- "Gate before execution" (concrete action)
- "Compliance-ready proof" (actionable benefit)
- "Real use case example" (evidence-based)

## List Maintenance

### Remove from campaign if:
- Hard bounce (invalid email)
- Unsubscribe
- Reply with "not interested" or "remove"
- Already a customer (move to "customer retention" campaign)

### Add to campaign if:
- From Stripe App Marketplace installs
- From demo bookings
- From your email list (with consent)
- From partner referrals (with permission)

### Compliance
- Include unsubscribe link in every email footer
- Honor unsubscribe requests within 10 minutes
- Keep records of consent (compliance with CAN-SPAM, GDPR, CCPA)
- Never share email list with third parties
