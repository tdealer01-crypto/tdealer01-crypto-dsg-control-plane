# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 3, 2026

DSG ONE Control Plane ("Service", "we", "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Account Information:** Name, email, organization name, workspace details
- **Configuration Data:** API keys, service credentials, integration settings
- **Usage Data:** Commands executed, decisions made, agent actions taken
- **Communication:** Support tickets, issue reports, feature requests

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **System Data:** Server logs, error traces, performance metrics
- **Access Data:** IP address, user agent, timestamps of API calls
- **Service Data:** Runtime metrics, execution traces, policy decisions
- **Analytics:** Feature usage patterns, audit trails

### 1.3 Third-Party Integration Data

When you integrate DSG ONE with other services (GitHub, Slack, Stripe, Supabase, etc.), we receive and process:
- Authentication tokens (encrypted at rest)
- Webhook data from integrated services
- Event notifications and callbacks
- Limited scope data from authorized integrations only

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

- **Service Delivery:** Operating the platform, processing requests, executing governance gates
- **Verification & Compliance:** Audit trails, evidence collection, regulatory compliance
- **Improvement:** Analyzing usage patterns, identifying bugs, optimizing performance
- **Communication:** Sending service updates, security alerts, support responses
- **Legal Compliance:** Meeting applicable laws and regulations
- **Security:** Detecting fraud, protecting against abuse, enforcing policies

## 3. Data Storage & Security

### 3.1 Storage Location

Data is stored in:
- **Primary:** Supabase PostgreSQL (US region)
- **Backups:** Encrypted backups in multiple regions
- **Logs:** Cloud provider infrastructure logs with encryption at rest

### 3.2 Security Measures

- **Encryption:** All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Access Control:** Role-based access control (RBAC) with principle of least privilege
- **Authentication:** OAuth 2.0, JWT tokens, API key management
- **Audit Logging:** Immutable audit trails of all data access
- **Dependency Management:** Regular security updates, automated vulnerability scanning
- **Code Review:** All changes reviewed before deployment

## 4. Data Retention

- **Account Data:** Retained while account is active; deleted 90 days after termination
- **Usage/Audit Logs:** Retained for 2 years for compliance and forensics
- **API Keys:** Stored encrypted; deleted upon rotation or account deletion
- **Backups:** Retained per standard 30-day rotation policy

## 5. Data Sharing

We **DO NOT** sell your data. We share information only when:

- **Required by Law:** Legal process, government request, court order
- **Service Providers:** Stripe (payments), Vercel (hosting), Supabase (database) - under DPA
- **Integrations:** Third-party services you explicitly authorize
- **Aggregate Analytics:** De-identified, aggregated statistics (no PII)

All third-party processors use data only for providing services and are contractually bound to protect your data.

## 6. Your Rights

### 6.1 You Can:

- **Access:** Request a copy of your data via support@dsg.pics
- **Correct:** Update or fix inaccurate information in your account
- **Delete:** Request deletion of your account and associated data
- **Export:** Export your data in standard formats
- **Opt-Out:** Disable certain data collection (where applicable)
- **Restrict:** Limit how your data is used

### 6.2 How to Exercise Rights

Email **privacy@dsg.pics** with:
- Your name and account email
- The specific right you're exercising
- Any supporting documentation

We will respond within **10 business days** (30 days for complex requests).

## 7. Cookies & Tracking

- **Session Cookies:** Used for authentication and session management
- **Analytics:** Optional analytics with user consent (Vercel Analytics, PostHog)
- **Third-Party:** GitHub, Stripe, and Supabase may set their own cookies per their policies
- **Opt-Out:** You can disable non-essential cookies in account settings

## 8. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
- Know what personal data is collected, used, and shared
- Delete personal information (with exceptions)
- Opt-out of sales/sharing of personal information
- Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

DSG ONE does not sell personal information. To exercise these rights, contact privacy@dsg.pics.

## 9. EU Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, we process personal data under:
- **Legal Basis:** Legitimate interest, contract performance, legal compliance
- **DPA:** Data processing agreements in place with all vendors
- **Rights:** Access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection
- **DPO:** Data protection inquiries to privacy@dsg.pics

## 10. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover such collection, we will delete it immediately. Users must be 18+ or have parental consent.

## 11. International Data Transfers

Data may be transferred to and stored in countries outside your residence. By using our Service, you consent to such transfers under appropriate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses, Privacy Shield equivalents).

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be announced via:
- Email notification (for registered accounts)
- In-app notification
- Updated "Effective Date" on this page

Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes.
