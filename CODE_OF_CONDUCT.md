# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for all contributors, users, and community members.

## Our Values

- **Respect:** Treat everyone with dignity and consideration
- **Inclusivity:** Welcome people of all backgrounds and experiences
- **Transparency:** Be honest and open in communication
- **Integrity:** Act with honesty and uphold ethical standards
- **Collaboration:** Work together toward shared goals

## Expected Behavior

We expect all community members to:

- Use inclusive language and be respectful of different perspectives
- Give and accept constructive feedback graciously
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards other community members
- Respect others' time, opinions, and contributions
- Acknowledge mistakes and learn from them

## Unacceptable Behavior

We do not tolerate:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech based on identity
- Threats, intimidation, or bullying
- Unwelcome sexual attention or comments
- Doxxing, sharing private information without consent
- Trolling, spamming, or disruptive behavior
- Insults or derogatory comments
- Abuse of power or authority
- Violations of privacy or confidentiality

## Reporting & Enforcement

### Reporting

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it to:
- **Email:** conduct@dsg.pics
- **GitHub Issues:** Use "Report" label (private, visible to maintainers only)

Include:
- Description of the incident
- Participants involved
- Date and location (if applicable)
- Any supporting evidence

### Response Process

1. **Acknowledgment:** We will respond within 48 hours
2. **Investigation:** We will investigate the report thoroughly
3. **Action:** Appropriate action will be taken (warning, suspension, removal)
4. **Follow-up:** We will check in with the reporter and respondent
5. **Appeals:** You may appeal decisions to conduct@dsg.pics

### Confidentiality

We will keep reports confidential and only share information on a need-to-know basis.

## Consequences

Violations may result in:
- Warning or request to apologize
- Temporary or permanent ban from community spaces
- Removal from contributor/maintainer status
- Legal action (in severe cases)

Severity of consequences depends on:
- Nature and severity of the violation
- Frequency of violations
- Impact on the community
- Good faith vs. intentional harm

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- GitHub repository (issues, PRs, discussions)
- Official communication channels (email, Slack, Discord)
- Community events and meetings
- Official social media accounts
- Unofficial spaces where DSG ONE community gathers
